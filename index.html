Her Beauty ❤️

Your eyes hold galaxies,
your smile calms my storms,
your hair flows like poetry,
and your beauty rewrites my universe.

👀 Her Eyes

Her eyes shine softer than moonlight, yet stronger than every emotion inside me.

💇 Her Hair

Every strand dances like silk in the wind, making my heart stop for a second.

🌸 Her Nose

Cute, delicate, beautiful — like every detail was crafted by love itself.
